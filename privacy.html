Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21/02/2026

Session Switcher respects your privacy.

1. Data Collection

Session Switcher does NOT:

  • Collect personal browsing history
  • Collect AI chat content
  • Track user activity outside supported sites
  • Sell or share personal data

The extension stores account session data locally in your browser to enable account switching functionality.

2. Payment Information

Payments are processed by our third-party payment provider. We do not store or process credit card information.

3. Analytics

We may collect minimal anonymized usage data to improve the product.

4. Data Storage

All account session information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome storage APIs.

We do not operate our own user database for personal content.
