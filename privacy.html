Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22/02/2026

Session Switcher respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our software.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Session Switcher does not:

  • Collect personal browsing history
  • Collect AI chat content
  • Transmit account credentials to external servers
  • Sell or share personal data

2. Local Storage

To provide account switching functionality, the extension stores session-related data locally in your browser using Chrome storage APIs.

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Payment Information

Payments are processed by our authorized Merchant of Record. We do not collect, process, or store credit card or payment information.

The Merchant of Record may collect personal data necessary to process transactions in accordance with their own privacy policy.

4. Limited Usage Data

We may collect minimal anonymized technical data (such as extension version or error logs) to improve product performance. This data does not identify individual users.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect the integrity of the extension. However, no software can guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the product constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
